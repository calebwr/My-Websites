Hello fellow Union College students, faculty, and staff. Our offices are in the Dick Building room 503. Once you walk out of the elevator, turn to your right and our door is straight forward. We are here to help you with any computer related problems. Our hours are Monday-Thursday 8am - 5:30pm and Fridays 8am - noon. Also we will not be in our offices between 12 and 1 pm for our lunch break.

We offer many services in Information Systems. We have programmers, network technician, and desktop technician who are all very talented group of people. Our network technicians will configure switches and vlans. They can figure out why your ethernet connection is not working. They also can diagnose the problem with the wifi. The desktop technicians update computers and monitors. They also install televisions and projectors in classrooms.

You can contact us by calling our number at (402)-486-2511. If you need something fixed or the internet is not working, then you can put in a ticket into our ticket system by going to IS Ticket. Once you arrive there, click on open a new ticket and fill out all the required spaces. After that click create ticket and your ticket will be read and worked on by our amazing IS staff. If we have not replied to you in the ticket please call us or come up to fifth floor and ask our secretary the status on the job that you put into the system.
